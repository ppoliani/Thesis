1 Create a Trip Student/Staff
Database

A use case where authenticated users can add trips that they have made, by providing the appropriate details.

2 Check Identity Database

A process that assures that some operations can only be executed by authenticated users

3 Edit a Trip Student/Staff
Database

A use case where an authenticated user (i.e. a student or a member of the staff) can modify the details of a trip that she has created

4 Login Student/Staff

A use case where users provide their credentials in order to enter the system and be able to execute operations that require them to be authenticated

5 view individual report Student/Staff

A use case where authenticated users (i.e. students or members of the university staff) can view their individual carbon emissions report.

6 View Carbon Footprints per Trip Database

A use case where authenticated users (e.g. students and members of university staff) can view the carbon emitted by each of the trips they have made.

7 View monthly/weekly/anual carbon footprints Database

A use case where authenticated users (e.g. students and members of university staff) can view the carbon emissions on weekly, daily or annual basis.

8 View Trends over Time Database

A use case where authenticated users (e.g. students and members of university staff) can view their carbon emissions over the course of time.

9 View Carbon Footprints per Trasport mean Database

A use case where authenticated users (e.g. students and members of university staff) can view the carbon emissions categorized by the transport means that were used.

10 View Group Report

A use case where authenticated users can view the report of the groups that they are members.

11 View University Report

A use case where any user can view carbon emissions report about a university.
