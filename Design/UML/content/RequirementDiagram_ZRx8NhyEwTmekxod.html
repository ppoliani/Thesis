Model Elements
Name Documentation
  Trip Creation : Requirement 		The system shall allow a student or a member of the staff to add a new trip, by providing the appropriate information, such as, source and destination of the trip. as well as, the transport mean that was used. A user must be authenticated before adding a new trip.
  Trip Correction : Requirement

The system shall allow a student or a member of the university staff to edit a trip that he has already created. This action demands the user to be authenticated.

  View Individual Report : Requirement

The system shall allow a student and a member of the university staff to view his individual report presenting his carbon footprints. The report shall present:

  1. individual carbon emissions per trip
  2. individual carbon emissions on monthly/weekly/annual basis
  3. individual carbon emissions per transport mean
  4. individual carbon emissions trends over the course of time

The report shall be accompanied by appropriate visualization elements (e.g. charts etc.).

Both students and members of the university staff shall be authenticated.

  View Group Report : Requirement

The system should allow a student and a member of the university staff to view carbon emission report, concerning the groups that they are member of (e.g. research groups etc.).

The details of the report are specified in the requirement "View Individual Report"

Both students and members of the university staff shall be authenticated.

  View University Report : Requirement

The system shall allow any user to view a summary report about the carbon emissions of the entire university.

The details of the report are specified in the requirement "View Individual Report"

Users need not to be authenticated
